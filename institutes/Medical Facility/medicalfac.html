Medical Facilities



Medical Facilities are available for every student in the campus completely free. Medical facilities from renowned doctors who also teaches our medical students.

,

Isolation Ward



Isolation ward is available for students facing any viral, bacterial, fungal diseases with proper facilities.

Intensive Care Unit



5 separate ICU are registered for campus students only.

Operation theatre



5 separate OT are registered for campus students only.

First Aid



First-aid section is available for campus students 24 hours.

,

Counselling Cell



The Institute has constituted a structure of Institute Counselling Services Cell (ICSC) for effectively handling issues like emotional difficulties, academic deficiencies, health and physical debility, interpersonal issues, career, and psychological matters etc. and headed by a Faculty-in Charge to extend empowerment to the Counselling Cell and to extend professional counselling services.
