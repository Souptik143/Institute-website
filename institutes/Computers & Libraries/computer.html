Computer Laboratories



Computer Labs are located beside laboratory. Computer labs are for students to print out important documents, register for anything, making presentations and for other important works.

Each computer is available with GTX2160 GPU, Amd Ryzen-5 7th Gen 8th core multi thread processor, 8gb DDR4x2 ram with 512gb SSD and with 110mbps lan port for each.

Computer labs are maintained under 25oC to ensure smooth performance under controlled air conditioners. Computer labs are available 24hours with scanners and HP printers.
