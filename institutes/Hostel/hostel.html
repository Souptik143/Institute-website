Hostel



Hostels are separated for each academic courses. Also hostels are separated for boys and girls each to ensure security. Each hostels have guards infront of gate and backside. Cards for entrance in hostel is present at entrance of each hostel. For now 15 buildings of each having capacity 500 students.

,

Hostel rooms



There is single and double occupancy rooms with no attach bath.. But on the availability of single rooms, students will be provided. We can't ensure 100% chance for single occupancy room. But students will be alloted hostel rooms.

Rooms have bed (with mattress and pillow), a window, study table, chair, wardrobe and shoerack. There will be a tubelight, fan, dimlight. 2charging points and a lan-port will be provided

Rooms don't have air conditioners, curtains. Students can bring pillow cover, bed cover, curtains, extension chord according to their use.

Hostel shower room



Each floor in each hostels has been provided with 2 hostel shower rooms at each diagonal corners. Each hostel shower has 4 bath cubes, 3 toilet sections for male in boys hostel, 3 toilet sections in female style in female hostel and 4 latrine areas with 1 in western style for both male and female.

Bath cubes are available with rack and hanger only which are used only in cubes. Hostels has been provided with daily cleaning and ventilation also.

Mess and Dining area



Basically there is three mess and dining areas for each academic students all provided with same quality food. Each mess has maximum of 500 seating capacity to overcome rush hour. Plates and glasses are available there in mess. Mess charge has been provided here. Additional charges for custom order may be taken and also on basis of availability.

Dance hall



Dance hall is there for each hostel at the basement for entertainment, practice or creating your own youtube content.

Dance hall has been also provided with audio systems, light and sound effect, background scenery.

Theatre



Small movie theatres with free movie-shows premiering only at weekend days from 6pm slot and 10pm slot only.
