Seat Reservation

  • Scheduled Caste (SC) – 10% of seats
  • Scheduled Tribe (ST) – 5.5% of seats
  • Other Backward Classes belonging to the Non-Creamy Layer (OBC-NCL) – 10% of seats
  • Persons with Disability (PwD) with at least 40% impairment – 2% of seats (supernumerary)
  • Kashmiri Migrants — 1 seat per IISTM course (supernumerary)
  • Economically Weaker Section (EWS) — up to 10% (as per Government of India rules)

Ranking System

  • All students have to attempt Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Three separate rank lists are made based on performance.
  • Every student has to clear individual subject and a minimum marks cutoff of 60 total average.
  • There will be a total of 30 questions, section A- 20 questions all are compulsory, and section B- 5 questions have to be attempted out of 10 questions for each subject.
  • Total marks will be 400 with each subject marks being 100.
  • For one correct answer, +4 will be awarded, and for each wrong answer, -1 will be deducted.
  • For BSMS rank list, total marks of Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and Biology will be taken.
  • For BTech rank list, total marks of Physics, Chemistry, and Maths will be taken.
  • For MBBS rank list, total marks of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology will be taken.
  • Not all students are provided with a rank, and also getting a rank does not ensure getting a seat in college.
  • Counselling system also goes according to your preference.
  • If you get a seat for your preference 4, then you will not get a seat for preference 5, although you are eligible with your rank.
  • Preference list will be provided during admission.
